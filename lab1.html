About Me

Hi, my name is Ellen Cheong and I am a photographer!

a deeper exploration of light, composition, and storytelling. As a primarily self-taught photographer with studio experience,
I have honed my craft through dedication and curiosity. My meticulous attention to detail allows me to create vivid
and dynamic images that capture the essence of each moment and reveal the innermost beauty of my subjects.
